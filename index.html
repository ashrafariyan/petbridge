The Process

Adoption made simple & safe

We built the platform we wished existed. Every step is designed to protect both the pet and everyone involved.

01
🔐

Create & Verify

Sign up and complete our quick ID verification. Takes 2 minutes and ensures everyone on PetBridge is who they say they are.

02
🔍

Browse Listings

Filter by pet type, age, location, and more. Every listing shows vaccination records, vet notes, and owner verification status.

03
💬

Message Safely

Connect directly with owners through our secure in-app messaging. No phone numbers shared until you're both comfortable.

04
🏡

Meet & Adopt

Arrange a safe meeting, sign our digital adoption agreement, and welcome your new family member home. That's it!

Safety First

Pets deserve to go to the right home

Our verification process isn't bureaucracy — it's our promise to every animal that passes through PetBridge that they'll be safe.

  • ✓
    Government ID Check Every user verifies their identity before listing or contacting a pet owner.
  • ✓
    Vet Records Encouraged Owners can upload vaccination and health records — shown as a badge on listings.
  • ✓
    Adoption Agreement A lightweight digital agreement gives both parties a clear, shared commitment.
  • ✓
    Report & Review System Community ratings and a clear reporting system keep bad actors out.
